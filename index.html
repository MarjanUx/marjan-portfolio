My Design Process

01

Discover

User interviews, surveys, and desk research to understand the real problem. I listen before I sketch.

02

Define

Synthesise findings into personas, journey maps, and a clear problem statement that guides every decision.

03

Design

Wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes in Figma, with accessibility (WCAG) baked in from the start.

04

Validate

Usability testing with real users. Iterate based on insights, not assumptions. Collaborate closely with developers.
